The combinatorics design conclusion

Question: How to hold all facets without operator crank?

Answer:

  1. `field_combinatorics.py` — endpoint math
  2. `field-plate-combinatorics-bridge.py` — meld→posture
  3. `field-compatibility-layers.py` — six layers, auto pipeline

Combinatorics became background physics. Layers became interface. Seals became trust. Diagnostic became kindness under failure.
