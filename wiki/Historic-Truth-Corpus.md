# Historic Truth Corpus

**Motto:** No new claim passes without historic truth corroboration — KNOWN seals, Lie Librarian witnesses, detective corpus anchors.

---

## Rule

Any information or new information must pass the historic truth corpus **before**:

- fielding claims in counsel
- witness acceptance
- library CLEAR verdict

Module: `lib/hostess7-historic-truth-corpus.py`
